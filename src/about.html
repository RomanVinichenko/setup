What are our traditions?

We commit to our traditions!

  • Generosity
    Our ancestors used to host their guests for 3 full days before they asked them about the purpose of their visit!

    At Setup, and as Saudis in general, we strive to follow the path of our ancestors in their giving nature and generosity. We even replicated their model for hosting our guests!

  • Dependability and Commitment
    Our reputation and credibility are our capital! We invest in trust with our clients and partners through strictly committing to our policies, timelines and commitments.

    This is reflected in our Refund Commitment Policy and in everything else we do.

  • Long-term friendships
    The values of friendship and long-term mutual benefit drive our innovation and service provision. We will help you achieve quick wins, cut down your costs and maximize your profits.

    However, we believe that the maximum profit comes from long-term relationships. That is why our approach to business is built on achieving win-win relationships for our clients, partners and team.
